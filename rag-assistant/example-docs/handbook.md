# Acme Co. — Employee Handbook (excerpt)

## Working hours
Standard working hours are 9:00 to 17:00, Monday to Friday. A flexible start
between 8:00 and 10:00 is allowed with manager approval.

## Remote work
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements
require director approval.

## Vacation
Full-time employees receive 28 days of paid annual leave per year, plus public
holidays. Up to 5 unused days can be carried over into the next year.

## Expenses
Business expenses are reimbursed within 14 days of submitting a receipt through
the expense portal. The daily meal allowance while travelling is 40 EUR.
