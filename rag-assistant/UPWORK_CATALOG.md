## Project description
Turn your documents into an AI assistant that **answers questions in plain language —
with sources.** Share your files (PDF, text, Markdown, exports) and get a private
assistant that searches them semantically and answers **grounded only in your content** —
every claim cited, no hallucinated facts.

**Great for:** company handbooks & SOPs · product documentation · research libraries ·
support knowledge bases · contracts · reports.

**How it works:** your documents → a semantic index (embeddings) → the assistant
retrieves the most relevant passages → an LLM (Claude or OpenAI) answers using *only*
those passages, with citations. If the answer isn't in your documents, it says so.

**You get:** a working assistant (command line **+ a clean chat web UI**), full source
code, and your data stays under your control.

## FAQ
**Is my data private?** Search runs locally on your machine; only the final answer step
calls the LLM with your own key. Your documents aren't sold or shared.

**Will it make things up?** No — it answers *only* from your documents and cites the
sources. If the answer isn't there, it tells you.

**Which file types?** PDF, text, Markdown — more on request.

**Claude or OpenAI?** Your choice, your key. Defaults to Claude.
